Vision and Mission

Vision:

To impart knowledge and excellence in Civil Engineering and technology with global perspectives to our students and to make them ethically strong engineers to build our nation.

Mission:

  1. To produce Civil Engineers of high caliber, technical skills and ethical values to serve the society and nation.
  2. To make the department a centre of excellence in the field of civil engineering and allied research.
  3. To provide knowledge base and consultancy services to the community in all areas of civil engineering.
  4. To promote innovative and original thinking in the of budding engineers to face the challenges of the future.

Objective of the Course


Civil enginering is professional engineering discipline that deals with the design,construction,and maintenance of the physical and naturally built environment,including public works such as roads,bridges,canals,dams,airports,sewerage systems,pipelines,structural components of buildings,and railways.
